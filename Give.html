Give

First Fruit

At Revival Fire Missions, we cherish the biblical principle of offering the first fruits of our labor to God. The concept of first fruits represents a heartfelt act of gratitude and trust in God's provision. By dedicating the first portion of our blessings to Him, we acknowledge His faithfulness and sovereignty in our lives. We believe that as we honor God with our first fruits, He blesses the remainder of our resources and endeavors. Through this act of obedience and worship, we align our hearts with God's purposes and open ourselves to His abundant blessings. Let us joyfully present our first fruits to the Lord, knowing that He will multiply our offerings and use them for His glory and kingdom.
